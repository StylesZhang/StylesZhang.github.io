### The University of Hong Kong

**DATA8017: [Fundamentals of Autonomous Intelligent Systems](https://datascience.hku.hk/study/research-postgraduate/data8017/)**  
Teaching Assistant

This course introduces the fundamentals of algorithms, data, and systems for autonomous intelligent systems, with applications in autonomous driving and robotics. The course covers topics including imitation learning and reinforcement learning, and includes discussion/tutorial/hands-on sessions.
